Karen

Karen also has a good selection of international schools, shopping centers, restaurants, and recreational facilities. Overall, Karen is a popular choice among ex-pats and wealthy locals, and many people find it to be a great place to live in Nairobi.

Runda

Runda provides homes that are well-developed, well-maintained, and safe. Private corporations provide communal utilities, such as water supply, which is provided by Runda Water Limited, an organization of residents. This promotes consistent service delivery and a higher living condition.

Muthaiga

At the turn of the 20th century, the area that is today known as Muthaiga was part of the Karura Forest, which was regarded as sacred ground by the Kikuyu people. The name Muthaiga refers to a tree whose bark was used as medicine by the Kikuyu.

Gigiri

Gigiri Homestead is a beautiful residence that provides comfortable, convenient and quality accommodation. It is situated within the lovely suburb of Gigiri and is within walking distance of the Village Market shopping centre, UN Head Quarters and US Embassy.

Kilimani

Kilimani is located between Kileleshwa and Lavington. It is characterised and famously known for the Nairobi Arboretum that sits close to State House Road.

Lavington

Lavington is known for some of its popular attractions, which include: Circle Art Gallery. Lavington Mall. Mikono The Refugee Crafts Shop.
